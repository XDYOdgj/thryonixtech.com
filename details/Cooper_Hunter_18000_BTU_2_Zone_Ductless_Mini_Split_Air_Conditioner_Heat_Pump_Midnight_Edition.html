Cooper & Hunter 18,000 BTU 2-Zone Ductless Mini Split System

Experience the perfect blend of advanced climate control and modern elegance with our 2-zone ductless mini split air conditioner. Ideal for homeowners and offices seeking flexible and efficient cooling and heating solutions.

Efficient Climate Control

The Cooper & Hunter 2-zone system provides powerful climate control with 18,000 BTU cooling and 12,000 BTU heating capabilities. Its high 25.1 SEER2 inverter system ensures exceptional energy efficiency, helping you save on electricity bills without compromising comfort.

Modern Design Meets Functionality

Enhance your space's aesthetic with sleek Midnight Edition indoor units that offer a modern black finish. These units operate quietly and efficiently, making them perfect for multi-room temperature optimization while maintaining a refined appearance.

Smart Home Integration

Enjoy effortless control with the built-in smart features of this 2-zone split system. Manage each indoor air handler independently via the Cooper & Hunter remote app or your smartphone, giving you complete control over your home's comfort.

Easy Installation for Better Performance

This system comes with a complete 25ft installation kit, including pre-flared and insulated copper line sets, communication wires, and drainage extensions. Designed for professional installation, it ensures a hassle-free setup and reliable performance.

Versatile and Reliable Climate Solution

Whether you're looking to manage temperatures in different rooms or need a reliable source of heating and cooling, this ductless mini split air conditioner delivers excellent performance. With advanced heat pump technology, it adapts to seasonal changes and maintains a consistent, comfortable environment throughout your space.
