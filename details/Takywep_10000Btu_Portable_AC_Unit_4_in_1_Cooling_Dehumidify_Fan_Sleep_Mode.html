Takywep 10000Btu Portable Air Conditioner

Experience powerful cooling for up to 450 sq. ft with the Takywep 10000Btu portable air conditioner. Designed to deliver fast and efficient comfort in any room, this unit is ideal for keeping your space cool during the hottest days of the year.

With its 4-in-1 functionality, the Takywep includes cooling, dehumidifying, fan, and sleep mode to give you ultimate control over your indoor environment. Whether you're working from home, relaxing, or sleeping, it adapts to your needs for a more comfortable experience.

The advanced hyperfilter technology removes dust, odors, and harmful gases from the air, creating a cleaner and healthier atmosphere for you and your family. This portable AC unit ensures that your breathing is always fresh and safe, no matter the season.

Installation is effortless with the included window kit and exhaust hose. The built-in remote control allows you to adjust settings from anywhere in the room, keeping you cool without having to move. Its sleep mode operates at less than 50dB, ensuring a peaceful night's rest on your budget.

Enjoy added convenience with the 1-24H timer and automatic condensate removal. These features help you manage energy usage efficiently while maintaining a consistently comfortable and quiet indoor climate. The energy-efficient R32 refrigerant ensures eco-friendly cooling and keeps your bills in check.

Perfect for homes, offices, or any space where comfort matters most.
