SereneLife SLPAC406W Portable Air Conditioner

Get the perfect balance of cooling, dehumidification, and air circulation with the SereneLife SLPAC406W. This portable air conditioner is designed for versatile use, offering three modes: Cooling, Dehumidifier, and Fan to suit your comfort needs, whether you're relaxing at home, working in your office, or enjoying a movie in the theater.

With 14000 BTU cooling power, the SereneLife SLPAC406W ensures effective temperature control for rooms up to 400 square feet.
