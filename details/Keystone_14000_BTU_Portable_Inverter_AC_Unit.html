Keystone High-Efficiency Portable Inverter Air Conditioner

Experience exceptional cooling in your home with this powerful and energy-efficient portable AC unit. Designed for medium-sized rooms up to 550 square feet, it combines strong cooling performance with smart technology to keep your space comfortable and cool with less energy use.

This portable air conditioner features 14,000 BTU ASHRAE cooling power, ensuring rapid and effective temperature control. With 12,000 BTU DOE energy efficiency, it reduces power consumption and lowers your electricity bills. The inverter technology provides smooth motor speed adjustment, delivering quiet operation and precise climate management for a peaceful, cool environment.

The washable air filter ensures cleaner indoor air and includes a built-in clean filter alert to remind you when it's time for maintenance. This keeps the unit running smoothly and efficiently. And with its portable design and built-in window venting system, it's easy to install and move between rooms, making it a versatile solution for your home's cooling needs.
